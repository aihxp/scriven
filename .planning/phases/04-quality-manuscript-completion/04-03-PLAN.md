**Create originality-check.md** (QUAL-10, per D-02):
- YAML frontmatter: `description: Scan drafted prose for AI-generated patterns and unintentional similarity to published works.`
- Usage: `/scr:originality-check [N]`
- Instruction: Role is "originality analyst". Load config.json, CONSTRAINTS.json, and drafted prose
- Per D-02, two scan categories:
  1. AI-generated pattern detection -- scan for: hedging phrases ("it's worth noting", "it's important to remember"), balanced lists (pros always matched with cons), abstract/vague language where specificity is expected, transition phrases that sound like essay writing ("furthermore", "moreover" in fiction), emotional telling over showing ("she felt sad" vs showing sadness), excessive qualifiers ("quite", "rather", "somewhat"), overly even paragraph lengths, lack of distinctive rhythm
  2. Published work similarity -- flag passages that echo well-known published works in phrasing, imagery, or structure. Note: this is heuristic (the AI identifies familiar-sounding passages), not a plagiarism database lookup
- Per D-02: "Surface concerns, don't block. Present findings as advisory, not as errors. The writer decides what to address."
- Output: For each finding show the passage, the pattern type (AI-generated or similarity), the specific concern, and a suggested revision if applicable. Include a summary assessment: "X passages flagged for AI patterns, Y passages flagged for similarity. Overall originality assessment: [strong/moderate/needs-attention]"
- Save to `.manuscript/{scope}-ORIGINALITY-REPORT.md`
